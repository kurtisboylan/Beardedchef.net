Chicken Parmesan

Photo by jeffreyw

A super easy, super delicious slow cooker Chicken Parmesan.

Serves up to 4 people.

Bearded Chef Recipe

Tweet

Ingredients

  • 2-4 chicken breasts, boneless and skinless
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 beaten egg
  • 1-2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • jarred marinara sauce or see meatballs for great homemade sauce

Procedure

  1. Cover the bottom of your slow cooker with the oil. In a bowl beat the egg. In a larger bowl mix the parmesan cheese, salt, pepper and breadcrumbs.
  2. Dip the chicken breasts into the egg being sure to coat the entire breast in egg. Then dip it straight into the dry mixture coating it in the bread crumbs and parmesan. Place each chicken breast at the bottom of the slow cooker.
  3. Place the mozzarella cheese on top of the chicken breasts and cover them in the sauce.
  4. Leave to cook for about 5 hours on low or until chicken is cooked. Serve on your choice of pasta.
