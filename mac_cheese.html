Mac and Cheese

Photo by Michael Stern

A rich and creamy mac and cheese with bacon, because everyone loves bacon.

Serves up to 6 people.

Bearded Chef Recipe

Tweet

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked macaroni
  • 2 1/2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups Cheddar cheese, grated
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ½ cup of bacon
  • 3 cups milk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • a pinch of paprika

Procedure

  1. Cook macaroni according to the package directions. Drain. Place bacon on a baking tray and place in oven at 200° until cooked.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Sprinkle in the flour stirring continuously until thick. Add the milk slowly while stirring. Stir the cheese into the mixture and cook on low heat melting the cheese and thickening the sauce.
  3. Cut the bacon into chunks and mix it into the macaroni. Pour the macaroni into a casserole dish and pour the sauce over it and stir.
  4. In a skillet melt the butter and brown the breadcrumbs. Once browned spread the breadcrumbs over the macaroni. Top with paprika and put in the oven for 30 minutes at 175°. Remove and enjoy.
