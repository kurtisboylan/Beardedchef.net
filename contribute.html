Contribute

Want to see your recipes on Bearded Chef?

At Bearded Chef not only do we want to give good people good food but we also want to try your good food and help you share your recipes with other Bearded Chefs. So if its breakfast, lunch, dinner, dessert or even a smoothie or cocktail, send your recipe in to us and and you will be able to share it with others in no time. Who knows you might just have the recipe that will get one of our bearded brothers out of the dog house or become the king of the block party.

What do we need to upload your recipe?

You have seen how our recipes are laid out. All we need is:

  • A list of ingredients with measures
  • A step by step procedure
  • A name for the recipe
  • Your name for credit
  • A picture or video of the finished product (optional)
